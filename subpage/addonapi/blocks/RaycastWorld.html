    TraceResult? ZVox.AddonAPI.RaycastWorld(universe univ, Vector pos, Vector dir, integer steps, boolean killOOB, integer groupMask)
    View source

    Description

    Performs a raycast on a given universe.

    Arguments

    1. universe univ
      Universe to raycast in
    2. Vector pos
      Origin position
    3. Vector dir
      Direction to raycast in, must be normalized
    4. integer steps
      How many steps shall we take at most
    5. boolean killOOB
      Whether to kill raycasts that are out of bounds or not
    6. integer groupMask
      Mask of what we can hit

    Returns

    1. TraceResult? ret
      Raycast result, nil if missing any of the arguments
